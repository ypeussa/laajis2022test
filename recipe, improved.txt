Pancakes:

Flour - 650g
Milk - 0,75L
Honey - 20g
Vanilla sugar - 10g
Table sugar - 200g
Egg - 2
Butter - 50g

Bake at 150c for 30mins until golden brown and cooked all the way through

Test with a toothpick, see if anything sticks, cook untill you get a clean toothpick through.

Let sit for a bit, cut, enjoy.
